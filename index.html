The process for
every project.

Structured, transparent and client-centered — ARCHAUS guides every project from concept through completion with precision.

Concept & schematic design.
We begin with deep listening and site analysis. Schematic design translates your brief into architectural concepts — floor plans, massing studies, and early 3D explorations that shape the project vision.
Design development & documentation.
With the concept approved, we develop detailed construction drawings and specifications. Every material, detail, and system is precisely documented — so builders have everything they need to build exactly what we designed.
Permitting & regulatory approval.
We navigate the full permitting process on your behalf — managing submissions, responding to city comments, and coordinating with structural and MEP engineers to achieve approval efficiently.
Construction administration.
During construction, we make regular site visits, review contractor submittals, answer RFIs, and ensure the building is being built to the letter of our drawings — protecting your vision to the very end.
